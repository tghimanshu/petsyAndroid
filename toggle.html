Dog’s nose is an indicator of health. Increased body temperature can make the dogs nose go dry. A checkup is required to check any underlying issue.

Eating of poop is called as coprophagy. It happens in a condition called as pika. There is a calcium and phosphorous imbalance in the body which leads the dogs to eat its poop to fulfill their dietary requirements

Dogs staring at owners can have different indications. Either they want something, food, treat or it can be your time too, or it can happen in aggressive breeds too

Grass helps to settle down the acids in the stomach. Most of the time dogs regurgitate or vomit to remove the excess acid after eating grass

Bathing depends on the climate zone you stay in. ideally once weekly to once in 15 days is fine
